TempVoiceChat
A simple voice chat application made in Javascript. This application requires no account whatsoever, and is completely FOSS (Github). It never stores ANY of your data, including names, servers, IPs, calls, or anything.
All you have to do in order to call with your friends is click the create server button, copy the server code, and have your friends input it and click join. Before calling, you must allow the site access to your microphone.
In order to change your name, simply edit the name field in the top right corner, and you can click the microphone and headphones icons to mute and deafen respectively.
That's all! Have fun chatting!
